My story


I'm an ex-waitor and I loved working as one but I lost my passion for it through time, people change :). I was always interested in tech and thought of working in some part of it but I didn't know what was possible. I saw an ad for a course for UX design one day at work, and thought "This is something I might actually be interested in!".


So I enrolled in the course and it actually opened up my mind to what's possible, that I actually could succeed if I put my mind to something. See, I wasn't always using my mind, I usually let life control me. That changed when I started reading and setting goals for myself.
